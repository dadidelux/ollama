Overview

Primoris has a long history of providing Industrial Construction Services throughout the U.S. and Canada, with safety and quality as our highest value proposition. We self-perform and deliver turnkey construction and EPC services to our clients within the refining, gas processing, product storage and transportation, chemical/petro-chemical, power generation, renewables, mining, pulp and paper industries. Primoris forms a partnership with our customers and major stakeholders from early-stage engineering through construction completion and commercial operation, safely, on-time, and within budget.

Energy

Primoris Services Corporation - Specialty Construction & Infrastructure > Capabilities > Energy
  • Energy
  • Utilities

Primoris' Energy and Renewables Segment provides a broad range of experience and capabilities for the engineering, procurement, and construction of projects in the downstream and midstream oil, gas, chemical, power, LNG, Hydrogen and renewables markets. We have extensive experience in utility scale Solar, Energy Storage, Renewable Fuels, Renewable Natural Gas, and Biomass Gasification. Primoris is a full-service, self-perform contractor with highly skilled, multi-disciplined construction capabilities offering EPC project delivery to energy infrastructure end markets. As a world-class engineering and construction services provider, Primoris is at the forefront of pioneering the fuel transition and energy transformation to a wide variety of customers in the Energy market.
